Cabbage Rolls (Sarmale)

Description

"All tourists who cross the threshold of our country, want to eat at least once traditional sarmale.
Their delicious taste and the secret of their preparation is very well known by our housewives, who always manage to prepare the best sarmale.
Below we tell you, step by step, how to make the best traditional sausages according to grandma's recipe."

Preparation times

  • Preparation time: 60min
  • Cooking time: 120min
  • Ready in: 180min

Ingredients

  • 2 kg sour cabbage
  • 450 g minced pork and beef
  • 50 g rice
  • 200 g onion
  • oil for frying
  • 100 g smoked ribs
  • 2-3 tbs broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt
  • pepper

How to prepare (Let's cook!)

  1. Peel the cabbage from the outer leaves and the stalks and pick out the leaves in which to wrap the meat.
  2. Peel and wash the onion, then chop finely. Place in a saucepan with oil and a little water and cook until golden.
  3. Mix the fried and cooled onions in a bowl with the meat, pepper, salt and rice, which has also been browned and cooled.
  4. Take walnut-sized mixture and place it on a cabbage leaf, rolling the edges inwards.
  5. Trim the smaller cabbage leaves and chop into small pieces to form a layer for the sausage.
  6. In a tall saucepan, place the sausage and bacon on top of the chopped cabbage and top with another layer of chopped cabbage.
    Pour the broth soaked in water over them, add the bay leaves and a few peppercorns. Simmer for 2 hours. Add water from time to time to prevent sticking.
  7. When the sausages are ready, sprinkle greens on top and they are just right to eat. You can have a portion of traditional sausages with sour cream or a hot pepper.
